Malala Yousafzai

Achivements:

  • Malala has written a book about her experiences called "I Am Malala". The book has since become a New York Times best-seller.
  • Malala has won a Noble Peace Prize
  • Malala's story has become a house-hold name, helping bring awareness of the inequality of women and girls in other countries.

    • Biograpahy:

    “ I tell my story not because it is unique, but because it’s the story of many girls” ~Malala

    Malala is a young woman who has been through many challeges just because she stood up for womens rights. Malala was born in Pakistan where women are not treated or seen as equals to men. Malala's father ran a school for girls which Malala attened. After, the takeover of the Taliban in Malala's village, there were many rules that were in placed one being girls were not allowed to go to school. Malala decided to speak out on behalf of herself as well as the other girls she went to school with and because this it made her a target to the Taliban.One October day Malala was shot in the head from an extremist, her story is about survival, bravery, and determination.

    • Malala's Goals:

  • Today, Malala goes around the world and meets women and girls with similar stories as hers. She hopes that their stories are heard too, to help show how women and girls are treated in other countries.
  • Malala has created a foundation where all the proceeds go to educators and activists around the world. The more education on this topic of women inequality in other countries, hopefully more work will be done to help protect young women and their right to learn.
